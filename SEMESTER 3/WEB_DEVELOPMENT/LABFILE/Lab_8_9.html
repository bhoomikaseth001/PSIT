CONSERVATION OF ENDANGERED SPECIES

Here is a list of endangered species from different categories, including mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and marine life. These species face various threats such as habitat loss, poaching, climate change, and pollution.

Mammals:

Amur Leopard (Panthera pardus orientalis)

  • Estimated population: fewer than 100 individuals in the wild.
  • Habitat destruction, poaching.

Sumatran Orangutan (Pongo abelii)

  • Estimated population: around 14,000 individuals.
  • Threats: Habitat loss due to deforestation, illegal pet trade.

Vaquita (Phocoena sinus)

  • Estimated population: fewer than 10 individuals.
  • Threats: Bycatch in illegal gillnets, habitat loss.
